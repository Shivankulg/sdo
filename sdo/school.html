About Us

SD Public School Shikhshan Sasthan is a premier educational institution committed to providing quality education with emphasis on academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth.

Founded in 2005, our school has grown into a respected institution known for its innovative teaching methods, dedicated faculty, and state-of-the-art infrastructure.

Our vision is to nurture responsible global citizens who excel not only in academics but also contribute positively to society through their knowledge, skills, and values.

Admissions

Admission Process

SD Public School welcomes applications from prospective students for the academic year 2025-26. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and fair.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Primary School: Minimum 5 years of age as of March 31, 2025
  • Transfer Admissions: Submission of previous academic records

Required Documents:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Records (for transfer students)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Address Proof
